## Image Requirements

### Horoscope Images
- Size: 800x600px
- Format: JPG
- Style: Cosmic, mystical, zodiac-themed
- Colors: Match zodiac sign colors

### Blog Article Images
- Size: 1200x630px (for social sharing)
- Format: JPG
- Style: Professional, astrology-themed
- Colors: Blue, purple, cosmic themes

### Zodiac Sign Images
- Size: 200x200px
- Format: PNG (transparent background)
- Style: Clean, modern zodiac symbols
- Colors: Individual zodiac colors

### Cosmic Backgrounds
- Size: 1920x1080px
- Format: JPG
- Style: Space, stars, aurora, galaxy
- Colors: Deep blues, purples, cosmic themes
